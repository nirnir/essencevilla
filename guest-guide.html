§ 02The pool, steam room & spa

Indoor · 12 mHeated · 28 °C

The pool

  1. The pool is open all hours; lighting is on a sensor and dims after midnight.
  2. Climate panel by the door: keep humidity below 65% — the dehumidifier is automatic.
  3. The robot runs at dawn — it is friendly; please don't try to swim with it.
  4. Towels are in the cedar chest by the entry. Use one per swim.
  5. Please shower before entering the pool — the rain head is set into the wall opposite the steam room.
If you would like the pool warmer or cooler, ask Inês — she can adjust between 26 °C and 30 °C.
Wellness wing · Eucalyptus~45 °C

The steam room

  1. Press ON on the panel by the door — it takes 12 minutes to reach temperature.
  2. Add a capful of eucalyptus oil to the reservoir below the bench.
  3. Maximum 25 minutes per session — drink water first.
  4. Auto-off after 60 minutes regardless.
A rain shower is set into the stone wall opposite — ideal between sessions.
Garden floorOpen all hours

The private gym

  1. Treadmill, Peloton, free weights to 30 kg.
  2. The Peloton tablet signs in to your account — sign out before you leave.
  3. Music: pull the Sonos zone GYM from the kitchen tablet or any phone on the network.
Daily, dawnCleaning robot

The pool robot

  1. Runs automatically at 6:30 am.
  2. If it has stopped, lift it out by the handle and place on the deck — Inês will reset it.
  3. Please do not redirect its programme; it has a complicated relationship with the steps.

§ 03The kitchen

The range

  1. Turn the dial inward and depress for 3 seconds to ignite.
  2. The left oven is gas (faster), the right oven is electric (more even).
  3. Always turn off the gas valve at the wall when the kitchen is unattended overnight.
A wood-fired oven is set into the outdoor loggia — Inês can light it on request, an hour ahead.

§ 04Televisions & sound

Samsung · Apple TV · Sonos

Every room has it. Every room hides it.

  1. One remote per room

    The Apple TV remote controls everything — TV power, volume, source. The Samsung remote is in the drawer, for the rare occasion it isn't enough.

  2. Sign in, sign out

    Each Apple TV is signed out between stays. Sign in with your own Apple ID, Netflix, Disney+, Prime. We do not see your credentials.

  3. Sonos — house-wide

    Twelve zones. Pull the Sonos app on any phone; the network appears as Ehrlich-Essence. The pool, terrace, and steam room have their own zones.

  4. Cinema mode in the library

    The library houses a 75″ panel and a soundbar; hold Menu on the Apple TV remote for 2 s for cinema audio.

§ 05Xbox & play

The playroom is for whoever lays claim to it first — by age or by guile.

  1. Powering on

    Press the Xbox button on the controller. The TV will switch to the right source automatically. A second controller is in the cabinet drawer.

  2. Your account

    Sign in to your own Xbox account if you want your games library. Otherwise, the house library contains FIFA, Forza, Minecraft, and a handful of family co-ops.

  3. Board games & books

    Below the TV, a small library of board games and children's books. We replenish quietly; please feel free.

§ 06The iRobots

Two robotic vacuums — Pedro and Inês — run nightly while you sleep.

  1. They live by the laundry

    Each on its dock. Please do not move the docks; they navigate by them.

  2. To pause them

    Press the round button on the top. They will rest until pressed again.

  3. If one is lost

    Look behind couches and under low tables. Please return it to its dock — it will recover within an hour.

§ 08Departure

Check-out is 11:00. We keep it light — Inês takes care of the rest.

  1. 10:30Sign out of all televisions and the Peloton (Apple ID, Netflix, Xbox).
  2. 10:40Return all keys to the bowl on the entry console; close pool gates.
  3. 10:45Turn off La Marzocco at the back; close the wall gas valve.
  4. 10:50Used towels in the bath; bedding may be left as it is.
  5. 11:00Front door is auto-locking; the gate closes behind you.

Late check-out is often possible — please ask Inês the morning before.
