Folio II · The House

A house of wood, light
& long horizons.

Six bedrooms. Seven baths. A heated indoor pool, a steam room, a private gym. A vaulted living room that opens to the garden, a kitchen built for long meals. Sleeps twelve, comfortably; designed for a single party of friends, family, or both.

Six bedrooms, each with its own light.

The South Suite

The largest master suite — king bed in soft boucle, two walls of sheer linen onto the south garden, walk-in dressing, and an ensuite with rain shower and double basin. The room where the sun arrives first.
