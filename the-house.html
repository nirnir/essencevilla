Six bedrooms, each with its own light.

I.

The Garden Suite

The largest master suite — king bed in soft boucle, two walls of sheer linen onto the garden, walk-in dressing, and an ensuite with rain shower and double basin. The room where the sun arrives first.

On the garden floor, with its own doors onto the covered porch and the indoor pool just beyond. A separate annex

The Guest House

A self-contained guest house in the garden, with its own front door. A full kitchen with a wood-fired grill, a bathroom, and a glass-walled living room that opens onto a deep covered terrace — the valley laid out beyond. The sofa opens to a double bed. For staff, grandparents, or guests who like their own key.
